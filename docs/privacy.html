Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Zentility ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Zentility Discord bot (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

2.1 Discord Information

  • Your Discord User ID
  • Your Discord username and avatar (only when you use commands)
  • Server IDs where you use the bot
  • Channel IDs for notification delivery

2.2 Roblox Information

  • Your Roblox User ID
  • Your Roblox username
  • Your Roblox presence data (online status, current game playing)
  • OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens (encrypted)

2.3 Usage Information

  • Command usage logs for debugging and improvement
  • Error messages and crash reports
  • Polling timestamps for presence monitoring

3. Use of Data

Zentility uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To monitor and analyze the usage and trends to improve user experience
  • To detect, prevent and address technical and security issues

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4.1 Token Encryption

All Roblox OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256-CTR encryption before being stored. Your passwords are never stored in any form.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you Services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by using the /unlink command.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with:

  • Discord - For bot functionality and notifications
  • Roblox - For user presence data via official APIs

We only transmit necessary data to these services and do not control their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through Discord.
